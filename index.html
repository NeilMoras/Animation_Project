Welcome to the Dragon Ball Z Fight Scene

This is a short semi-interactive game based on the famous Anime cartoon Dragon Ball Z. It is themed on a short fight scene between Frieza, Vegeta and Goku(Kakarot). Frieza was defeated by Goku before but he has come back trying to boast his new next level Golden form and ready to take on a revenge. Vegeta and Goku are the only Saiyans left from their race as their planet was destroyed by Frieza with a SuperNova long time ago. Frieza is now on a mission to wipe out planet earth. Goku has recently recovered from a fight and is getting ready to get back to his full potential.

Game Instructions

The game consists of two scenes, The Starting Dialogue Scene and the Main Scene. Keep on clicking on the "Start Scene" button gently to activate each dailogue at a time on each click. The "Main Scene" will follow right after the "Start Scene". There is no ending for it yet, but stay tuned as it is a work in progress!
